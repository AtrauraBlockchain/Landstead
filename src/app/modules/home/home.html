LANDSTEAD

Land and property registry

EXPLORE

Landstead uses NEM blockchain technology to create a register of land and other property, allowing government and citizens to co-create an open blockchain system that can be trusted and consulted by interested parties.

NEM is used since it offers all services for a project like this, such as payments, messaging, asset management, multi-user accounts and a naming system implemented with an outstanding core blockchain technology.

The Process

Submit a citizen account

As Citizen initiate the registration process by submitting your account for government validation.

Validate an account

As Civil Servant validate a citizen account after verifications, such as the citizen ID.

Register a Property

As Cadastral Service register a property based on its unique identification, together with additional information about the property.

Register property ownership

As a Cadastral Service register the ownership of a property, linking it to the account of the owner(s).

Verify your account

As Citizen open your account and see your possessions to verify registered ownership of property.

Transfer property

As a Notary transfer a property from previous owner(s) to new owner(s).
