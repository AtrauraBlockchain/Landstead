LANDSTEAD

Land and property registry

LEARN HOW IT WORKS

"Landstead - Land and property registry on NEM is one of the best proposal I've seen in this competition, it shows how the creator of the system has a wholestic view of things."

Landstead uses NEM blockchain technology to create a registry of land and property, allowing government and citizens to co-create an open blockchain system that can be trusted and consulted by interested parties.

NEM provides the perfect foundation for this project, offering payments, messaging, asset management, multi-user accounts and a naming system implemented with an outstanding core blockchain technology.

The Process

Submit a citizen account

As a Citizen, initiate the registration process by submitting an account for government validation.

Validate an account

As a Civil Servant, validate a citizen account after verifications, such as the citizen ID.

Register a Property

As a Cadastral Service, register a property based on its unique identification, together with additional information about the property.

Register property ownership

As a Cadastral Service, register the ownership of a property, linking it to the account of the owner(s).

Verify your account

As a Citizen, open an account and verify registered ownership of properties.

Transfer property

As a Notary, transfer a property from previous owner(s) to new owner(s).

The Benefits of using Nem Blockchain

Security

Built on a secure blockchain environment, the Landstead platform puts security first with NEM at its core.

Control

Governments are the axis of the platform, managing user privileges as well as land and property data. Through notaries and government servants we manage veracity via the blockchain.

Social

We give the citizen the capacity to manage and visualize the properties they have, emphasising their prominent role on the Landstead platform.

Scalability

Created to manage high quantities of simultaneous users without loss of performance.

Efficiency

The Landstead platform is created specifically to offer users the flexibility to operate whenever they choose.

Auditable

Users are able to review any transaction and registration on the NEM blockchain, providing transparancy and veracity.
