LANDSTEAD

Land property build on Nem blockchain

EXPLORE

Landstead uses NEM to create a register of land property on the blockchain, controlled by the government in collaboration with citizens. Allowing to have a personal digital account that shows the own,transfer and register of properties.

NEM is a peer to peer platform that offers services like payments, messaging, asset making, and naming system with an outstanding core blockchain technology.

The Process

Register your account

Go to an official government building and with the help of an police officer register your account

Validate your account

The police officer then will check your ID with your account to validate it.

Register your Building

With the register of the property (in paper) go to and official government building to transfer it to the blockchain.

Link the property of your building

The government will send the property of the registered building to your account.

Check your properties

Entering in your Landstead account you can check the properties related to you.

Transfer your property

With the help of a notary and the consent of the government you can transfer properties to other citizens
