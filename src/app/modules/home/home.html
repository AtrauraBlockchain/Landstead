LANDSTEAD

Land and property registry

EXPLORE

Landstead uses NEM blockchain technology to create a registry of land and other property, allowing government and citizens to co-create an open blockchain system that can be trusted and consulted by interested parties.

NEM is used since it offers all services for a project like this, such as payments, messaging, asset management, multi-user accounts and a naming system implemented with an outstanding core blockchain technology.

The Process

Submit a citizen account

As Citizen initiate the registration process by submitting your account for government validation.

Validate an account

As Civil Servant validate a citizen account after verifications, such as the citizen ID.

Register a Property

As Cadastral Service register a property based on its unique identification, together with additional information about the property.

Register property ownership

As a Cadastral Service register the ownership of a property, linking it to the account of the owner(s).

Verify your account

As Citizen open your account and see your possessions to verify registered ownership of property.

Transfer property

As a Notary transfer a property from previous owner(s) to new owner(s).

The Benefits of using Nem Blockchain

Security

Built on a secure blockchain environment, Landstead uses NEM as the core of his platform, we have worked on the platform so is based on “security first”.

Control

Governments are the axis of the platform, giving privileges to the users and managing the land property. By notaries and government servants we manage the responsibility of the veracity and transactions.

Social

We give the citizen the capacity to manage and visualize the properties they have, creating a main role for them in our platform.

Scalability

Created to manage a high quantity of users without losing performance

Efficiency

Platform is created in high disponibility for users to operate whenever they want.

Auditable

Users are able to review any transaction and registration in the NEM blockchain giving always veracity of operations.
